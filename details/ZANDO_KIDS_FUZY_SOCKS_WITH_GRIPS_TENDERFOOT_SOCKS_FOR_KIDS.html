Zando Kids Fuzzy Socks with Grips

Keep your child’s feet warm and snug with our Zando Kids Fuzzy Socks. Perfect for winter months or any time they need extra comfort, these socks are crafted to blend softness with practicality.

Featuring a unique grip design on the soles, these socks help prevent slipping on smooth floors, making them an ideal choice for safe indoor play and daily use. The lightweight, cushiony material ensures all-day comfort, while the soft fuzzy lining adds that cozy touch your little one will love.
