Designed with your child's comfort and safety in mind, YILLEU's waterproof jackets are the perfect companion for cold days and unpredictable weather. The breathable fleece lining keeps them warm, while the water-resistant outer shell ensures they stay dry. Whether it's a trip to the park, a day at school, or a family adventure, these jackets offer reliable protection.

Key Features

  • Built with durable, weather-resistant materials to withstand all conditions
  • Reinforced seams for added strength and longevity
  • Adjustable hood and band to ensure a secure fit for growing kids
